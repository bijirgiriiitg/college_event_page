Alcheringa

Alcheringa, also known as "Alcher", is the annual cultural festival of Indian Institute of Technology (IIT), Guwahati. Spread over 3 days and 4 nights, Alcheringa is conducted towards the end of January every year.

Techniche

Spread over three days and four nights, Techniche is generally conducted towards the end of August each year.

About Us

Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati, the sixth member of the IIT fraternity, was established in 1994. The academic programme of IIT Guwahati commenced in 1995.

Every year IIT Guwahati conduct many events in campus. Some of these events are given below.

  • Alcheringa
  • Techniche
  • Consulting & Analytics

Through these events students get oppertunity to show their talents and also enjoy in event like Alcheringa because the Alcheringa has become one of Asia's most significant college-level cultural events.
